Toast,

Coded Website

A interactive website exploring the idea of time extending beyond clocks, and how there are timers in our lives that come in various different forms, one of which being the act of waiting for our bread to toast. The user either successfully toasts their bread, or it burns and smoke starts to engulf—and ultimately destroys—the website.
