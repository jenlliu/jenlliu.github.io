Love Letter to Vietnam,

Coded Website
2024
A website created based on a film photograph of my mother at the beach in Vietnam that I had taken in 2022.

The website is a reflection of my time in Vietnam, making note of all the things and places I had taken for granted while being there. It aims to create an environment in which one can absorb themselves in, through the use of sound and movement.
