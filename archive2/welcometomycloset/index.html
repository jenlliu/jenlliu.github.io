Welcome to my Closet,
Spiral Bound Book,
2024
Welcome to my Closet is a visual narrative showcasing the clothes in my closet. It is an exploration of creative direction and print production; how images change from digital to physical.

Exhibted at Spitting Image Art Book Fair.
Creative direction by Jen Liu, in collaboration with Ngoc Vu.
