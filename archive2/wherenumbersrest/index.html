Where Numbers Rest,

Coded Website
2024
Where Numbers Rest is a book on the exploration of house numbers and urban planning within New York City. A focus on the economical reasons behind the grid, the neglected POC neighbourhoods in NYC, and cultural differences in house numbers in different cities.

The design explores creating a navigational system within the book itself, connecting the content to the images that exist on a separate piece of paper. The website acts as a digital translation of the piece.
