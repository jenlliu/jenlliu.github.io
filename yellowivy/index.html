Yellow Ivy,
Accordion Book,
2024
Yellow Ivy is an accordion book created as an exercise based on the words "ivy, yellow, life, town, barefoot." The drawings are of images I took on trains, hikes, and so on. The book is hole-punched to mirror the idea of sunlight through leaves.
